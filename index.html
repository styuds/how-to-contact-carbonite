Troubleshooting Common Carbonite Issues

Before contacting Carbonite support, try these troubleshooting tips for common issues:

Backup Problems

  • Backup not running: Check your internet connection and ensure Carbonite has proper permissions
  • Slow backup speed: Pause other bandwidth-intensive activities during backup
  • Files not backing up: Verify the files are in your selected backup folders and not excluded

Restoration Issues

  • Can't restore files: Check your available storage space on the destination device
  • Missing files in restore: Verify you're looking at the correct backup version
  • Restore fails: Try restoring to a different location or in smaller batches

Account and Login Problems

  • Can't log in: Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" feature
  • Account not recognized: Ensure you're using the correct email associated with your Carbonite account
  • Subscription issues: Check your payment method and verify subscription status
